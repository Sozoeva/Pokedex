Interesting facts about pokemon

Many Pokemon names are made up of numbers

All Pokémon have very unusual names. To come up with so many names, their creators had to strain their imagination a lot. However, not everyone knows that some names even consist of numbers.

Cubone wears the skull of his late mother

Sometimes the family history of some Pokémon is very creepy, and Cubone is a great example of this. He belongs to the earth type, and no one has ever seen his face because of the mask (an alien skull over the monster's head).

Slowpoke are considered a delicacy

Many people think that Pokémon are only made to fight other Pokémon. However, the truth is that Pokémon are also food. In the earliest versions of the game, Slowpoke tails cost a lot of money and were considered a real delicacy.

Raydon was the first Pokémon

Many will say that the first Pokémon was invented by the cute Pikachu or Bulbasaur, but according to Ken Sugimori (Ken Sugimori, character designer of the Pokemon video game), the first hero drawn by the creators of the concept was Raydon.

Munna even appeared in the first game

Although Munna was not introduced into the game until his fifth generation, there is a mention of a pink monster with flower patterns in the training session of the first generation of Pokémon, which matches the description of Munna.
